About Nepal Border

The Nepal border at Rupaidiha (Bahraich district) is one of the busiest India-Nepal land crossings, leading to Nepalganj — a major commercial city in western Nepal. Located approximately 180 km from Lucknow Airport, the border is a critical entry point for tourists heading to Lumbini, Pokhara, and Kathmandu, as well as business travellers and pilgrims.

Our Lucknow Airport to Nepal Border taxi service is tailored for international travellers needing seamless border transfers. Our drivers are familiar with immigration procedures, document requirements, and the exact drop-off location at Rupaidiha for a smooth cross-border experience.

Why Choose an Airport Transfer for Nepal Border?

Public transport to the Nepal border is slow and not foreigner-friendly. International tourists carrying luggage often face difficulties with multiple transfers.
