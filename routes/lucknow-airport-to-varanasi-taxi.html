About Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Banaras, is the spiritual capital of India and one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. Located on the banks of the holy Ganga River, 320 km from Lucknow Airport, Varanasi draws millions of Hindu pilgrims, international tourists, and seekers every year for spiritual experiences, Ganga aarti, and Kashi Vishwanath darshan.

Our Lucknow Airport to Varanasi taxi service is designed for travellers who want a comfortable, fixed-fare journey to the holy city without depending on uncertain public transport. Our drivers are well-versed with all temple routes, ghat locations, BHU campus, and the busy bylanes of old Varanasi.

Top Places to Visit in Varanasi

Kashi Vishwanath TempleOne of the twelve Jyotirlingas, the most sacred Shiva temple in India.
Dashashwamedh GhatThe main ghat famous for the spectacular evening Ganga aarti ceremony.
SarnathThe Buddhist heritage site where Lord Buddha delivered his first sermon.
Manikarnika GhatThe sacred cremation ghat believed to lead to moksha.
Banaras Hindu University (BHU)One of Asia's largest residential universities and a cultural landmark.
Assi Ghat & Tulsi Manas TemplePeaceful spots for sunrise meditation, boat rides, and serene darshan.
