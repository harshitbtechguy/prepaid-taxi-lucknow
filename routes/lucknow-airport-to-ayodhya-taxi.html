About Ayodhya

Ayodhya, the sacred birthplace of Lord Rama, has emerged as one of India's most visited pilgrimage destinations following the consecration of the magnificent Ram Mandir. Located 135 km from Lucknow on the banks of the holy Sarayu River, Ayodhya welcomes millions of devotees, tourists, and spiritual seekers every year from across India and abroad.

Travellers arriving at Lucknow Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport often choose Ayodhya as their immediate destination for darshan at the Ram Janmabhoomi temple. Our dedicated airport-to-Ayodhya taxi service makes this onward journey seamless, with experienced drivers familiar with every temple route, parking zone, and accommodation area in the holy city.

Top Places to Visit in Ayodhya

Ram Mandir & Ram JanmabhoomiThe grand temple at the birthplace of Lord Rama, a must-visit pilgrimage site for every devotee.
Hanuman GarhiA revered hilltop temple of Lord Hanuman, considered the guardian of Ayodhya.
Kanak BhawanAn exquisite temple gifted to Sita Mata by Kaikeyi, known for its beautiful idols and architecture.
Sarayu GhatThe sacred river ghat for evening aarti, holy baths, and serene boat rides.
Treta Ke ThakurAn ancient temple built on the site where Lord Rama performed the Ashwamedha Yagna.
Nageshwarnath TempleOne of the oldest Shiva temples in Ayodhya, established by Lord Rama's son Kush.

Why Choose an Airport Transfer for Ayodhya?

Public transport from Lucknow Airport to Ayodhya can be unreliable and time-consuming, especially for elderly pilgrims, families with children, and groups carrying luggage.
