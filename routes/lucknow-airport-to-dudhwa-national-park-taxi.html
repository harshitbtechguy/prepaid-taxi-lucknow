About Dudhwa

Dudhwa National Park, nestled along the India-Nepal border in Lakhimpur Kheri district, is one of India's premier tiger reserves and a UNESCO biosphere highlight. Located approximately 220 km from Lucknow Airport, Dudhwa is famous for its rich biodiversity — Bengal tigers, swamp deer, one-horned rhinoceros, and over 400 species of birds.

Our Lucknow Airport to Dudhwa taxi service is tailored for wildlife enthusiasts, safari travellers, and nature photographers. Our drivers are familiar with the Lakhimpur-Palia route, lodge locations, and entry gate timings for hassle-free safari trips.

Top Highlights of Dudhwa

Dudhwa Tiger ReserveHome to Bengal tigers, leopards, and over 38 species of mammals in dense terai forests.
Kishanpur Wildlife SanctuaryFamous for swamp deer (barasingha) and elephant herds, part of the larger Dudhwa landscape.
Katarniaghat SanctuaryKnown for Gharial crocodiles, river dolphins, and rare aquatic life.
Tharu Tribal VillagesCultural villages of the indigenous Tharu community near the park boundary.
Sathiana & Sonaripur RangesPopular safari zones for tiger and rhino spotting inside Dudhwa.
Bird Watching TrailsOver 400 bird species including hornbills, eagles, and migratory water birds.

Why Choose an Airport Transfer for Dudhwa?

Reaching Dudhwa via public transport is extremely difficult — there are no direct trains to Palia, and bus connections are slow and unreliable. A pre-paid airport taxi is the only practical option for wildlife tourists, especially when carrying camera gear and luggage. Our drivers understand safari timings and can adjust pickup schedules to match jungle entry hours.
