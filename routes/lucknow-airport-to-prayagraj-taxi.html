About Prayagraj

Prayagraj (formerly Allahabad) is one of India's most historic and spiritually significant cities, famous for the sacred Triveni Sangam — the confluence of the Ganga, Yamuna, and mythical Saraswati rivers. Located 210 km from Lucknow Airport, Prayagraj draws millions during the legendary Kumbh Mela and Magh Mela festivals, as well as year-round visitors for darshan and historical exploration.

Our Lucknow Airport to Prayagraj taxi service caters to pilgrims, history enthusiasts, and university visitors with a comfortable, fixed-fare AC ride. Our drivers know every important location in Prayagraj — from Triveni Sangam to Allahabad University.

Top Highlights of Prayagraj

Triveni SangamThe sacred meeting point of three holy rivers, central to Hindu pilgrimage.
Allahabad FortA Mughal-era fort built by Akbar overlooking the Sangam, with the Akshaya Vat tree inside.
Anand BhawanThe ancestral home of the Nehru-Gandhi family, now a museum showcasing India's independence history.
Hanuman Mandir, SangamFamous reclining Hanuman temple located near the sacred Sangam.
Allahabad UniversityOne of India's oldest universities, an iconic educational institution.
Khusro BaghA Mughal-era walled garden with historic tombs and beautiful architecture.

Why Choose an Airport Transfer for Prayagraj?

The 210 km journey to Prayagraj can be exhausting via public buses or trains, especially for elderly pilgrims and families.
