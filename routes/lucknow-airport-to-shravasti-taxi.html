About Shravasti

Shravasti is one of the most important Buddhist pilgrimage sites in India, located approximately 175 km from Lucknow Airport in Balrampur district. It was here that Lord Buddha spent 24 monsoon retreats and preached many of his teachings. The ancient ruins, monasteries, and meditation sites attract Buddhist pilgrims from Thailand, Sri Lanka, Japan, Myanmar, and across the world.

Our Lucknow Airport to Shravasti taxi service is designed for international Buddhist pilgrims, tourists, and history enthusiasts. Our drivers are familiar with the meditation centres, ancient ruins, and accommodation in Shravasti, offering a seamless airport-to-pilgrimage experience.

Top Highlights of Shravasti

Jetavana MonasteryThe famous monastery donated by Anathapindika where Buddha gave many sermons.
Saheth-Maheth RuinsArchaeological remains of ancient Shravasti city, with stupas and monastery foundations.
Ananda Bodhi TreeThe sacred tree said to have been planted from a cutting of the original Bodhi tree.
Angulimala's CaveSite associated with the famous bandit-turned-monk Angulimala.
Chinese, Thai & Sri Lankan MonasteriesModern Buddhist monasteries built by international Buddhist communities.
Sobhnath TempleImportant Jain pilgrimage site believed to be the birthplace of the 3rd Tirthankara.

Why Choose an Airport Transfer for Shravasti?

Shravasti has no airport and limited rail connectivity. International Buddhist pilgrims arriving at Lucknow Airport require a comfortable, reliable transfer for the 175 km journey.
