About Kanpur

Kanpur, often called the industrial capital of Uttar Pradesh and the Manchester of the East, is one of India's largest manufacturing and educational hubs. Located just 85 km from Lucknow Airport via the high-speed Lucknow-Kanpur Expressway, Kanpur attracts business travellers, IIT visitors, students, and tourists year-round.

Whether you are arriving for an IIT Kanpur conference, a leather industry meeting, or visiting family in Swaroop Nagar, our Lucknow Airport to Kanpur taxi service ensures a seamless, fixed-fare transfer.
