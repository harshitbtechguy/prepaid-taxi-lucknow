About Pre Paid Taxi Service

Pre Paid Taxi Seva Samiti is the authorized airport pickup service operating from Lucknow Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport (LKO/Amausi) since 1998. Over the past 25+ years, we have served thousands of travellers including business passengers, families, pilgrims, international tourists, and government officials — providing reliable, fixed-fare airport transfers without the hassle of meters, surge pricing, or bargaining.

Our pre paid taxi network covers all major destinations in Uttar Pradesh and North India, including Ayodhya (Ram Mandir), Kanpur, Varanasi (Kashi), Prayagraj (Sangam), Dudhwa National Park, Nepal Border (Rupaidiha), Gorakhpur, Agra, and Delhi. We also serve all local Lucknow city zones — Hazratganj, Gomti Nagar, Charbagh, Alambagh, Indira Nagar, and beyond.

Unlike app-based aggregators, we operate as a government-recognized airport pickup network with drivers physically waiting at the authorized airport pickup zone. This ensures that every passenger — whether arriving at 2 PM or 2 AM — finds a ready, professional driver with a name board and a clean AC vehicle.
